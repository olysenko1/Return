WELCOME

This is an interactive open source software tool intended to help you explore the stock market returns.

The main purpose of this application is to increase the level of financial litteracy among people and promote investments in the stock market. "Money makes money. And the money that money makes, makes money." Imagine you bought a couple of Facebook stocks during the IPO. How much would you have now? Never miss such opportunities again. "Investing should be more like watching paint dry or watching grass grow. If you want excitement, take USD800 and go to Las Vegas."

 WHAT YOU CAN CURRENTLY DO WITH THAT APPLICATION:

  • Explore the cumulative retun of the selected stock from the S&P 500 over the selected period
  • Check the VaR and ES risk measurement estimated using empirical quantile method. 

IMPORTANT: This application is at the development stage. More features are coming soon. Reach me out if there is anything you want me to add.
